When words matter most

  

By preparing and educating loved ones ahead of time, we make hard conversations easier.

Let's Talk: Hard Conversations

Emotional

Our emotional reactions to painful topics can be intense. This can end up drawing focus onto you rather than the person who needs your support. Our tool allows you to process your emotions ahead of time so you can be more emotionally present in the moment.

Uninformed

It can be hard to know what to say or what questions to ask. Without information and education ahead of time, you may end up saying something problematic or harmful without meaning to. Our tool gives you coaching so you know what to say to be most supportive.
