HOW IT WORKS ABOUT LOGIN

When words matter most, we are here to help, one conversation at a time.

How does Empowered Conversation work?

  1. When a survivor is ready to disclose about their assault, they go onto our website and input the cell phone number of the person they will disclose to.
  2. That person then receives a text message from our site that says someone they love wants to have a conversation with them about sexual assault and are provided with a link to a short, trauma-informed module on assault, how best to respond, and what to avoid so as not to re-trigger the survivor.
  3. Once the module is completed, a text is generated back to the survivor, letting them know the recipient is ready to listen.

That’s it. Now that the groundwork has been laid,
you can begin to have a meaningful conversation.
